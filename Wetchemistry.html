Wet Chemistry

The Great ESKAPE: Reisolating Natural Products From Fungal Extracts

University of South Florida: Baker Lab

Mar 2023 - Jul 2023

• Utilized methods including High- Performance Liquid Chromatography, Nuclear Magnetic Resonance, and Medium Pressure Liquid Chromatography to analyze compounds extracted from fungal samples.

• Determined structures of four compounds bioactive against ESKAPE pathogens.

• Future works include further purifying fraction CQ10-L to better elucidate structures of discovered compounds.

Investigation on Altering Properties of the Oxidation of Soy Sauce Wastewater with the Level of Chemical Oxygen Demand (COD)

Robinson High School International Baccalaureate Program

Feb 2018 - Sep 2019

• Conducted an independent study on the application of the Fenton reaction as a method to purify soy sauce wastewater.

• Concluded that an iron concentration within 30-50 mg/L best decreased COD value and color of soy sauce wastewater without adding additional pollutants.

• Future works include reducing instances of random and systematic errors as well as running additional trials.
